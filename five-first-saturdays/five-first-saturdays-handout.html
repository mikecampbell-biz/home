Five First Saturdays Devotion

On December 10, 1925, She appeared to Lucia and said,

I promise to assist at the hour of death, with the graces necessary for salvation, all who on the First Saturday of five consecutive months confess their sins, receive Holy Communion, recite five decades of the Rosary, and keep me company for fifteen minutes meditating on the mysteries of the Rosary, with the purpose of making reparation to my Immaculate Heart

How to Practice the Five First Saturdays Devotion

To fulfill the request made by Our Lady and the Child Jesus to Sister Lucia, one must do the following on the first Saturday of five consecutive months:

Practice Description
Confession Within 8 days before or after the Saturday, with the intention of reparation
Holy Communion Received on the First Saturday itself
Rosary Recite five decades
Meditation Spend 15 minutes with Mary meditating on the mysteries of the Rosary
Intention All acts must be done in a spirit of reparation to the Immaculate Heart of Mary

Suggested Prayer of Intention: O Most Holy Virgin Mary, I offer you this Communion in reparation for the offenses committed against your Immaculate Heart. Amen.

"I shall ask that on the First Saturday of every month Communions of reparation be made in atonement for the sins-of the world." ~Our Lady to Sister Lucia, July 13, 1917

"To all who do this, I promise to assist at the hour of death with the graces necessary for salvation." ~Our Lady to Sister Lucia, 1925

The Five Principal Offenses

  1. Immaculate Conception denied - Denial of Mary's unique grace, free from original sin -
  2. Perpetual Virginity denied - Claims that she was not virgin before, during, or after Christ’s birth -
  3. Divine Motherhood denied - Refusal to accept her as Mother of God and spiritual Mother of all -
  4. Contempt instilled in children - Leading young hearts away from love of Mary -
  5. Image desecration - Profaning or scorning holy representations of Our Lady -

Fatima Prayers

Title Given by Prayer
Eucharistic Prayer Interior Impulse O most Holy Trinity, I adore Thee! My God, my God, I love Thee in the Most Blessed Sacrament!
Pardon Prayer Angel of Peace My God, I believe, I adore, I hope and I love Thee! I beg pardon for all those that do not believe, do not adore, do not hope and do not love Thee. (Repeat 3x)
Angel's Prayer Angel of Peace O Most Holy Trinity, Father, Son and Holy Spirit, I adore Thee profoundly. I offer Thee the most precious Body, Blood, Soul and Divinity of Jesus Christ present in all the tabernacles of the world, in reparation for the outrages, sacrileges and indifferences by which He is offended. By the infinite merits of the Sacred Heart of Jesus and the Immaculate Heart of Mary I beg the conversion of poor sinners. Amen.
Sacrifice Prayer Our Lady O Jesus, it is for love of You, for the conversion of sinners, and in reparation for the sins committed against the Immaculate Heart of Mary.
Decade Prayer Our Lady O My Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fires of hell. Lead all souls to Heaven, especially those most in need of Thy Mercy.

Core Messages of Fatima

"Pray, pray very much and make sacrifices for sinners." ~ Our Lady, May 13, 1917

"You have seen hell where the souls of poor sinners go." ~ Our Lady, July 13, 1917

"Jesus wishes to establish in the world devotion to my Immaculate Heart." ~ Our Lady, June 13, 1917

"Say the Rosary every day to obtain peace for the world." Our Lady, All apparitions, 1917

"Tell everybody that God grants graces through the Immaculate Heart of Mary. They must ask for them from her." ~ Jacinta to Lucia, shortly before her death

"The sins which cause most souls to go to hell are the sins of the flesh." ~ Revealed to Jacinta by Our Lady

"Oh, if only people knew what eternity is, they would do everything to change their lives." ~ Jacinta

"I am going to heaven soon. You are to stay here and say that God wishes to establish in the world the devotion to the Immaculate Heart of Mary." ~ Her final mission entrusted by Our Lady

"If only I could put into the hearts of everyone the fire that is burning in my own heart and makes me love so much the Heart of Jesus and the Heart of Mary." ~ Jacinta (a mystical longing to share divine love)

“I want to console Hidden Jesus.” ~ Francisco (often spending hours in silent prayer before the tabernacle)

“I liked seeing the Angel, but I liked still more seeing Our Lady. What I liked most of all was seeing Our Lord in that light which Our Lady put into our hearts.” ~ Francisco
